Polarity - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Overview

Polarity ("the Game") is a free-to-play arcade game. Your privacy matters to us.

Data Collection

The Game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. Gameplay data remains stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The Game integrates with the following Google services:

  • AdMob — displays ads and may collect device identifiers
  • Play Games Services — manages leaderboards and user profiles
  • Play Billing — processes in-app transactions

Ad Consent & GDPR

For EEA users, the Game uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before serving personalised ads.

Children's Privacy

The Game targets users 13 and older and does not knowingly collect data from younger users.

In-App Purchases

The optional "Remove Ads" purchase is processed through platform app stores. Payment data is not retained by the developer.

Data Retention & Changes

Local data persists until the app is uninstalled or data is cleared. Policy updates will appear on this page with revised dates.
