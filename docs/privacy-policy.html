Polarity - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

Polarity ("the Game") is a free-to-play arcade game. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The Game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All gameplay data (high scores, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob - Serves ads within the Game. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide personalised or non-personalised ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Games Services - Provides leaderboard functionality. When you sign in to Play Games, your profile and scores are managed by Google. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing - Processes in-app purchases. Transaction data is handled by Google. Google Privacy Policy

Ad Consent (GDPR/EEA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the Game uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before serving personalised ads, in compliance with GDPR.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

In-App Purchases

The Game offers an optional "Remove Ads" purchase. This transaction is processed entirely by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to retain. Local game data persists on your device until you uninstall the Game or clear its data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
